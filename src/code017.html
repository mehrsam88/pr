the game awards 2023

The Game Awards 2023, held on December 7, 2023, was a night of celebration and recognition for the best video games of the year. The event was hosted by Geoff Keighley at the Microsoft Theater in Los Angeles, California, and featured a star-studded lineup of celebrities, musical performances, and world premieres. Game of the Year Baldur's Gate 3 took home the coveted Game of the Year award, cementing its position as one of the most critically acclaimed and commercially successful games of the year. After a decade of anticipation, Alan Wake 2 has finally arrived, delivering a chilling sequel that plunges players back into the darkness of Bright Falls. Baldur's Gate 3:A Grand Continuation

Larian Studios' Baldur's Gate 3 has been shaping up to be one of the most ambitious and exciting RPGs in recent years, and the Game Awards 2023 further cemented its position as a game-changer. The game took home the coveted Game of the Year award, as well as Best Role-Playing Game.
